Strength

MIRAGE-2000

Multi-role, single-engine 4th generation jet fighter powered by One Snecma M-53-p2 turbofans engine producing 9700 Kgf with reheat.Max speed is 1550 mph with a combat radius of 435 miles.

SUKHOI Su-30 MKI

Air superiority fighter with two seat long range interception and air standing patrol powered with Two Al-31 FP turbofans with TVC. Range of 3,000 km with internal fuel which ensures a 3.75 hour combat mission.

C-130J SUPER HERCULES

Tactical Airlift Transport have Four Rolls-Royce AE2100D3 turboprops engine. Max speed is 410 mph and max takeoff weight is 74393 kgs.

LCA TEJAS

Tejas is single-seat, single-jet engine, first Fly-by-wire (FBW) and multirole light fighter Aircraft designed by HAL under MakeInIndia. Smallest and lightest in its class of contemporary supersonic combat aircraft.

ALH

Utility helicopter developed by HAL. Can carry 12 passengers or 04 stretchers and fire missiles. First exported to Nepal and Israel. Designed to meet the requirement of both military and civil operators.

HAWK

Single-engine advanced jet trainer aircraft, powered with One Rolls-Royce Adour Mk.871, Twin Spool Turbofan engine. Max speed 1065 km/hr and max range 2520 km.

MiG-27

Single engine, single seater tactical strike fighter aircraft of Russian origin having a max. speed of 1700 km/hr (Mach 1.6).

Jaguar

A twin-engine, single seater deep penetration strike aircraft of Anglo-French origin which has a max. speed of 1350 km /hr (Mach 1.3).

MIG-29

Twin engine, single seater air superiority fighter aircraft of Russian origin capable of attaining max. speed of 2445 km per hour (Mach-2.3).

AN-32

Twin engine turboprop, medium tactical transport aircraft of Russian origin with a crew of five and capacity to carry 39 paratroopers.
